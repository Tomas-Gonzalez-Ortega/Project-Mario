This Mario project corresponds to the group milestone asked to perform during CPSCP 2030 Web Development II, class dicted by our beloved instructor Stephen Chiong. With his guidance we have achieved basics to submerge into the video game developing world with some of the main features: Coordinates, gravity, interface, response, sound and design.

Our job mostly consisted in creating a group representing most of the areas of a web development team, experiencing the pros and cons of working as a teamplayer, dividing the duties, setting deadlines and trying to solve issues within the schedule to keep the same flow these companies have to deal with on a daily basis. Sandeep and Harpreet spent several days trying to solve and debug programming aspects of the model and the API. While Tomas was mostly focused on the design, the web page and the sound of the prototype.

Instructions:
Use ↑ to Jump
Use ← to move left
Use → to move right
Use the space bar to shot fireballs
